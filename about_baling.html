Baling

Kedah, Malaysia


Baling is a town and district located in the state of Kedah, Malaysia. Nestled in the northeastern part of Kedah, Baling is known for its scenic beauty, lush landscapes, and historical significance. The area is characterized by its rolling hills, dense forests, and vibrant local culture. Visitors can explore natural attractions like the Baling Hot Springs and the Lata Bayu Waterfall, as well as engage in outdoor activities such as hiking and eco-tourism. Baling is also home to rich cultural heritage, with traditional crafts, local cuisine, and historical sites offering a glimpse into the region's past and present.
